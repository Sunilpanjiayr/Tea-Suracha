Common Tea Leaf Diseases

Black Root Rot

A fungal disease causing root decay and plant weakening.

Solutions:

  • Improve soil drainage
  • Use fungicide treatments
  • Practice crop rotation

Blister Blight

Causes white blister-like lesions on tea leaves, reducing photosynthesis.

Solutions:

  • Regular pruning
  • Apply copper-based fungicides
  • Maintain proper shade

Red Rust

Algal disease causing reddish-brown patches on leaves.

Solutions:

  • Improve air circulation
  • Remove infected leaves
  • Maintain optimal humidity
